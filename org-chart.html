Sales & Marketing Agent Org

A team of Claude Code subagents simulating a full B2B revenue organization

Start by addressing the CRO with a revenue goal — everything cascades from there
  • Entry Point
    CRO
    Start here
    CRO — Chief Revenue Officer Top-level revenue orchestrator. Owns the full number — new business, expansion, and retention. Coordinates vp-sales & vp-marketing. Every revenue goal and strategy decision starts here.
    • Leadership
      VP of Sales
      VP of Sales Owns quota setting, pipeline management, forecast accuracy, and team performance. Receives revenue targets from the CRO and translates them into executable plans. Manages JSM, Sales Ops, and Sales Enablement.
      • Management
        Sales Ops
        Sales Operations CRM hygiene, forecasting, pipeline reporting, process enforcement, and sales metrics. Use for pipeline audits, weekly forecasts, win rate, and deal velocity reports. Outputs to Context/reports/.
      • Management
        Sales Enablement
        Sales Enablement Battle cards, objection handling guides, talk tracks, call scripts, and onboarding playbooks. Use when reps need competitive intelligence tools or scenario-specific messaging. Outputs to Context/playbooks/.
      • Management
        Junior Sales Manager
        Junior Sales Manager Mid-level orchestrator. Coordinates SDR, Account Executive, and Account Manager. Manages leads through the full sales cycle, reviews team activity, and escalates blockers upward.
        • Execution
          SDR
          SDR — Sales Development Rep Owns top-of-funnel: company research, decision maker identification, buying signal monitoring, and cold outreach sequence creation. Hands off to Account Executive once a meeting is booked.
          • Specialist
            prospect-researcher
            prospect-researcher Researches target companies and identifies decision makers. Surfaces firmographics, buying signals, and competitive context. Outputs structured profiles to Context/prospects/.
          • Specialist
            outreach-writer
            outreach-writer Writes personalized cold email and LinkedIn sequences (5–7 touches). Reads prospect profiles from Context/prospects/ and saves sequences to Context/outreach/.
        • Execution
          Account Executive
          Account Executive Owns deals from first meeting through close. Handles BANT/MEDDIC qualification, proposal drafting, and deal progression. Receives handoff from SDR; hands off to Account Manager on close.
          • Specialist
            meeting-prepper
            meeting-prepper Prepares pre-call briefs and qualifies leads before meetings. Builds BANT/MEDDIC scoring and discovery questions. Outputs to Context/meeting-preps/.
          • Specialist
            pipeline-reporter
            pipeline-reporter Generates pipeline status reports, deal health summaries, stall flags, and weighted revenue forecasts. Reads deal data from Context/pipeline/; outputs to Context/reports/.
        • Execution
          Account Manager
          Account Manager Owns all post-close customer relationships. Manages renewals, identifies upsell and cross-sell opportunities, monitors churn risk, and maintains account health. Receives handoff from Account Executive.
    • Leadership
      VP of Marketing
      VP of Marketing Owns pipeline contribution from marketing: MQL targets, campaign strategy, content program, and brand positioning. Coordinates with VP Sales on MQL quality and handoff SLA. Manages Demand Gen and Content Marketing.
      • Marketing
        Demand Gen
        Demand Generation Manager Owns top-of-funnel marketing: campaign briefs, lead scoring, MQL definition, MQL-to-SDR handoff process, and campaign-to-pipeline attribution. Outputs to Context/campaigns/.
      • Marketing
        Content Marketing
        Content Marketing Manager Produces sales collateral, email nurture sequences, thought leadership content, one-pagers, case study drafts, comparison guides, and ROI frameworks. Outputs to Context/content/.
